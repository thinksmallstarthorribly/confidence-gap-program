# The Confidence Gap — Mini Program Portal

**What happens when capable people get stuck.**

A take-home resource and promotional tool for Alex Cooper's talk at the Stirling Business Association on April 30, 2026.

## Overview

The Confidence Gap is a framework that explores why capable people remain stuck — not because they lack ability, but because they lack the self-trust to use it outside the one place they built it. This portal serves as both a pre-event promotional page and a post-event reference for attendees.

## Framework Modules

| Module | Title | Focus |
|--------|-------|-------|
| 01 | The Silo | Where your confidence lives and why it does not travel |
| 02 | Perspective vs Perception | The cause and effect relationship between how you see and what you believe |
| 03 | Beliefs Under Pressure | Putting beliefs under pressure to extract something that approximates the truth |
| 04 | The Evolution | The N- to P+ journey — three stages of self-trust development |
| 05 | Building Capability to Respond | Responding to opportunity, challenge, and risk |
| 06 | Life's 3 Choices | Choose, collaborate, or defer — and what each one costs |
| 07 | You Are Here → Who You Are | The timeline model and accepting current circumstances honestly |
